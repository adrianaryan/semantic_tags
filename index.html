Wind City Pie

Now that deep-dish darling Windy City Pie has a permanent kitchen at Batch 206's new
Interbay tasting room, you can sit and enjoy one of Seattle's top pies while sipping a cocktail
from one of Seattle's top distilleries. You can still get an order to go, too, but you won't
have to pick it up at a random street corner as was formerly the case.

On a way to get some pie!
